Photo/Video Sources

All photos represented on the map are sourced from the #occupygezi Tumblr page. I take no credit for the photos used. I deliberately chose photos taken from the Tumblr page, as all of the photos were freely submitted by people who took those photos first-hand on the ground. I wanted to represent the mass diversity and proliferation of photos taken during the protests and shared on social media that were not seen in traditional media.
